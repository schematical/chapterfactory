Our Story:

Hello my name is Matt Lea creator of chapterfactory.com. I am an aspiring author and writer at Ship Or Get Off The Pot.

While doing the research for my second book Hacking Fear: Tips and tricks for helping high performing athletes through mental issues I learned a thing or two about creating reward/result loops that would enforce desired behaviors. I eventually decided to apply these principals to help other writers finish their books and so was born chapterfactory.com.

Our Mission:

To build tools that help aspiring writers write.
